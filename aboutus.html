About us and our company mission!





Our company mission is to provide quality clothing that is moderately priced for a large variety of people. Our products are geared towards people who like alternative clothing such as goth, victorian, steampunk, and other styles!

We want to make these products because there is not enough representation of different styles in the fashion industry. A lot of what is sold today is not considered alternative fashion, and is geared towards the "mainstream" audience. We decided to change that!
